What it's All About

Created for the food lovin' individual, Side Street Eats serves as a resource for the local foodie. Are you looking for alternatives to the same, boring chain stores/restaurants searches engines have to offer? Then this is the app for you. By bringing together the owners of local establishments and those in the area who consider themselves passionate about food, we are not only connecting the eater with the eatery, but the eatery with the eater.
